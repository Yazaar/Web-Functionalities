The game:

MrJump is a game where you control an aweosme green box which starts off on a static object. The goal is to reach the second static object which is positioned to the right of the starting position. There are two movable objects, one above you and one underneath you, use these to your help and jump on them to reach the other side. Drag and drop to move the movable objects to the perfect positions.

controls:

W: Jump A: Move left D: Move right Hold leftclick: Move the movable blocks
